# YPP ERP — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Mobile-first ERP for Yash Poly Pack, a plastic packaging manufacturer. Operators, QC staff, supervisors, HR, and directors use it daily on shop-floor smartphones to log batches, quality checks, GRNs, dispatch, calibration, complaints, KPIs, and compliance — replacing paper-based QMS records.

## Users
- **Operator** — logs batch start/params/defects on the shop floor; low literacy assumed
- **Supervisor** — oversees work orders, machines, daily production
- **Store (Storekeeper)** — manages GRN, RM stock, dispatch
- **QMR (Quality Manager)** — quality checks, calibration, NCR, CAPA, complaints
- **Director** — KPI dashboard, complaints, production overview
- **HR** — personnel and training records

## Brand
- Name: YPP ERP / Yash Poly Pack
- Primary color: Safety orange (#EA580C) — industrial, high-vis, authoritative
- Accent: Near-black (#0F172A) — serious, grounded
- Fonts: Barlow Condensed (display/headers), Barlow (body)
- Tone: Utilitarian and direct. No decorative flourish. Every pixel must justify itself on a dusty shop-floor phone.

## Anti-references
- No consumer-app softness (pastel, rounded blob shapes, friendly illustrations)
- No SaaS dashboard clichés (purple gradients, glass cards, hero metrics)
- No unnecessary animation — operators are working fast with dirty hands

## Register
product

## Strategic Principles
- Mobile-first, max-width 600px
- 8pt spacing grid
- Barlow Condensed for ALL labels, headers, badges — signals "this is a tool"
- Orange is reserved for primary actions and active states only — not decoration
- Tables over cards wherever data is tabular
- Slide-in panels (translateX) for forms — no modals
